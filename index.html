Hegemony NFT

Hegemony is a cyberpunk inspired collection of 7777 ultra-realistic 3D-rendered avatars that will provide you with exclusive access to our growing ecosystem.

Living on the Ethereum blockchain, each NFT will grant you access to an ever-increasing utility list backed by a network of experts. Holders will also be able to earn passive income by engaging with the various ecosystem’s mechanics. Join us and access a growing community with exponential real life value and benefits.

Hegemony seeks positive impact in the NFT industry through transparency and good business practice.

Learn & Earn

Our goal is to offer free courses in all matters related to Blockchain Technology, Financial Markets, Coding, Marketing, 3D Design, Animation, etc… and allow you to earn tokenized crypto rewards by engaging with our ecosystem. You can hold, trade or use these rewards on our oncoming marketplace to buy our upcoming Merch, NFTs, Whitelists, NFT conference tickets at discounted prices, and more. This Rewarding strategy will work through a Ticketing System. Meaning, you will be able to redeem and sell your one-time accesses for each Conference or Event, for free on our marketplace and generate income when you don’t participate. A double win for you as a holder, either you learn and earn, or you just earn.

Our ultimate goal is to build a Learning Hub for our holder where they can access Certified Educational Courses that will be backed and sponsored by IRL Universities, Consulting and Training Firms.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes hegemony unique ?


In addition to our high quality artworks, Hegemony is built by a professional team with extensive experience in consulting, trading, strategy, marketing and project management. Partaking in building a sustainable decentralized future, Hegemony aims to bring transparency, good business practices and real-life positive impact into the NFT ecosystem.

How to buy our NFT ?


There will be 3 main phases for you to get your very own NFT from our collection : Check our discord to learn more about it and find our beginner minting tutorial

Do I own the NFT after minting ?


Yes. It’s fully yours. In addition to the blockchain ownership, you can put it as a pfp, use it for your own profit, creating art, merchandise, logos, or even as a tattoo!

Where can I see my NFT ?


After minting, the NFT will automatically be dropped to your Etheurem wallet and you will be able to see it by either connecting to your metamask or to your Opensea account.

Are there secondary market royalties ?


Yes, a 7.5% royalty will be deducted from secondary sales and used in two main ways :

1) Funneled towards our liquidity pool to help increase the value of Hegemony’s token.
2) Towards our utilities and marketing campaigns to grow the project, hence the value of your NFT on the secondary market.
